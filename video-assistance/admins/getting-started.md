# Getting started with Video Assistance

This guide walks you through the essential first steps to set up and use Apizee Video Assistance.

---

## 1. Log in to the portal

If you received a login invitation, follow the steps in [Log in to the Apizee portal for the first time](log-in-to-the-apizee-portal-for-the-first-time.md).

---

## 2. Configure your service

Set up the service your agents will use to receive assistance requests:

1. [Create a new service](configuration-on-the-apizee-portal/create-a-new-service.md)
2. [Customize the tickets](configuration-on-the-apizee-portal/customize-the-tickets.md)
3. [Configure the agents](configuration-on-the-apizee-portal/configure-the-agents.md)

---

## 3. Send your first invitation

Once your service is configured, you can start a session by inviting a guest:

1. [Create a ticket and send a quick invitation by email and/or SMS](start-a-video-assistance/create-a-ticket-send-an-invitation/create-a-ticket-quick-invitation-by-email-and-or-sms.md)
2. [Receive an incoming assistance call](start-a-video-assistance/receive-an-incoming-assistance-call.md)
