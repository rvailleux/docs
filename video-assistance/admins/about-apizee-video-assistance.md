**Apizee for Video Assistance**, is a solution without installation which makes it possible to assist users, customers or technicians remotely through video assistance.
Read more about Apizee for Video Assistance [here](https://www.apizee.com/video-assistance-customer-support/)

With a single click, the solution gives the possibility of sending an invitation by SMS or email to a customer or an user.

Thanks to the link received, the user shares the smartphone camera with an agent or an expert who has direct visual access to the problem, as well as on site.

During the video interaction, the agent can use collaborative tools to highlight elements, give instructions to the customer thanks augmented reality and transmit files or documentation to reach a faster and more efficient resolution.

Use Apizee Video Assistance to help users and technicians remotely through real-time video. This page explains the key features and how to get started.
