#  Attach a report to the ticket

1. In the left-hand menu, click the service for which you want to attach a report.
2. In the ticket list, find the ticket you want to attach a report to and click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-edit.png) 
 
![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-service-assistance-edit-ticket-eac5eada.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The ticket page displays.
{% endhint %}
3. Go to the **Assignment** menu, in **Attach report**. 

| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/tip.png) | If your company configured a report template, this message displays: **You can download a report template** <br> - Click **report template**. <br><br> <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-ticket-page-download-report-template.png" alt=""> <br> <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png"> <span>The report template downloads. <br> </span> - Open the document, fill it and save it. |
    | --- | --- |
4. Click **Choose File** to import the report you just filled.
5. Click **Save**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-portal-Attaching-report-to-ticket.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The report is attached and display at the bottom of the **Ticket request** menu.
{% endhint %}
