# Looking for coworker assistance?

Thanks to the portal directory, you can chat or call someone (audio or video, it is up to you!) from the same company.

You can also create group conversations. Let's have a look on the main options:

* [Send a quick invitation](communicate-colleagues-send-a-quick-invitation.md)
* [Send a common invitation](communicate-colleagues-send-a-common-invitation.md)
* [Activate and turn off the microphone and camera](communicate-colleagues-activate-and-turn-off-the-microphone-and-camera.md)
* [Share files](communicate-colleagues-share-files.md)
* [Share a screen](communicate-colleagues-share-a-screen.md)
* [Take a picture and draw on it](communicate-colleagues-take-a-picture-and-draw-on-it.md)
* [Share the pointer](communicate-colleagues-share-the-pointer.md)
* [Record the session](communicate-colleagues-record-the-session.md)
