#  How to switch my camera during a video assistance?

{% hint style="info" %}
Available only if the participant has **several cameras** on their device.
{% endhint %}
{% hint style="info" %}
You are participating in an ongoing session as a guest and you want to switch your camera.
{% endhint %}

1. Tap one time on your video.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-guest-tap-video-stream.png)
2. Tap the **switch camera** button ![](../../.gitbook/assets/switch-camera-button.png)

![](../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-guest-tap-switch-camera-button.png)
3. Tap again until the right camera displays.

{% hint style="success" %}
Need to switch camera to display the [flashlight feature](turn-on-my-flashlight.md)? 
Tap the **switch camera** button until the **flashlight**  **** button displays in the upper left corner of the screen.
{% endhint %}

![](../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-guest-tap-switch-camera-display-light-button.png)
