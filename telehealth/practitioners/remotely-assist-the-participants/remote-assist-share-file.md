{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to help a participant to share a file.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-actions.png)
2. Click the **Guests** tab. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-actions-guests-tab-selected.png)
3. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu.
4. Click **Share file**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-menu-action-orga-guests-tab-share-file.png) 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | An invitation is sent to the participant and displays on his screen as follow: |
    | --- | --- |

 **Guest screen** 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-notif-asking-share-file.png) 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | When accepted, a file explorer opens on the participant screen. 
He needs to choose a file and click **Open**. 
Once downloaded, the files displays in the **Messages** tab. |
    | --- | --- |
