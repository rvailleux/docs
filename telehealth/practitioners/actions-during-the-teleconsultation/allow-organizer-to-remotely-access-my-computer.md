{% hint style="info" %}
Available only on computers with Windows 10 & 11. 
 [More information](../about-apizee-solutions.md#browser-remote-access) about compatibility.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
You need the organizer to help you and want him to remotely access your computer.
{% endhint %}

1. When the organizer sends you an invitation, click **Allow**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-view-allow-remote-access.png)
2. Follow the steps displayed on your screen:
    1. Click **Share the screen**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-view-remote-access-share-screen-click.png)
    2. Make sure you select the **entire screen**. Here is an example for Chrome Web browser: 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-view-remote-access-choose-entire-screen.png)
    3. Click **Download** to download the Apizee remote access software application. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-view-remote-access-download-app.png)
    4. Once it is completely downloaded, launch the application by double-clicking the file **.exe**.
    5. Accept the safety warnings displayed by Windows.
        * Click **More info   ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-view-remote-access-windows-instruction-more-info.png)**
        * Click **Run anyway**   
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-view-remote-access-windows-instruction-run-anyway.png) 

| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The remote access session starts. <br> The organizer sees your screen and can control your mouth and keyboard. |
            | --- | --- |

 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-guest-view-remote-access-starts.png)
3. When you are done, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-orga-remote-access-stop.png) 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-orga-view-remote-access-started-click-button-to-stop.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The remote access stops and the agent cannot control your computer anymore.
{% endhint %}
