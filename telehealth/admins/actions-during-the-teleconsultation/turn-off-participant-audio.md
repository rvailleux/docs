{% hint style="info" %}
You are a guest participating to an ongoing session. 
Someone's noise is bothering you and you want to cut this participant audio, but just for you.
{% endhint %}
1. On the top left of the participant video, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-participant-option.png)

![](../../.gitbook/assets/conf-participant-option-dots-click.png)
2. Click **Turn off audio** to cut the participant audio, just for you.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-dots-participant-option-turn-off-audio-guest-view.png)

    {% hint style="success" %}

    You do not hear anymore the participant. But the other participants still hear that person.

    {% endhint %}
3. Click the button again to activate the participant audio.
