#  Need to reinvite a guest, 
Change the invitation time or the participant contact?

1. On the left-hand menu, click the service your want.
2. On the right, click **List**. 
That will help you find faster the session you are looking for.
3. In the list, find the session you want and click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-edit.png) 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-teleconsultation-edit-teleconsultation-page.png) 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The page displays. |
    | --- | --- |
4. Scroll down the page and click **Edit**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-conf-edit-conference-page-edit-button.png.png)

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | This window displays. The following steps may be different whether you are an organizer or an assistant: |
    | --- | --- |

    | **Organizer** | **Assistant** |
    | --- | --- |
    |  -  You are the one who runs the session.    -  Check that your name displays under the **Organizer** title.  -  Enter the guest email address (2), or type the name of someone who is in the **Directory **(2a or 2b).   -  Enter the phone number, if necessary.   -  Click **Next**.   |  -  You do not run the session.   -  You want to entrust the organization to someone else.    -  Click **Change organizer**&#160;(1) and enter the name of the organizer in the search bar (1a).   -  Enter the guest email address (2), or type the name of someone who is in the **Directory **(2a or 2b).   -  Enter the phone number, if necessary.   -  Click **Next**.   |

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-create-session-invitation-delegation.png)
5. Select a **Duration**, a **Start time** and**End time** **Day** and **Hour**.
6. Choose a **Reminder**, if you want it.
7. Click **Next**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-create-session-planning.png)
8. Select a **template**. 

    | ![Information](../../.gitbook/assets/info.png) | If you did not create your own template, select the default template. |
    | --- | --- |

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/Apizee-see-also-icon.png) | **See also**[Customize the notification templates](../configuration-on-the-apizee-portal/configure-the-teleconsultation/customize-the-notification-templates.md) |
    | --- | --- |
9. Select a **language**.
10. Click **Next.   ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-create-session-preview-invitation.png)**
11. Give a name to the teleconsultation , if you want it.
12. If you chose to send this invitation by email, add a personal message. 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The message will display in the email. |
    | --- | --- |

 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-portal-create-session-finalize.png)
13. Click **Finalize**. 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The invitation is sent. |
    | --- | --- |

 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-page-info-t├йl├йconsultation.png) 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The guest receives a message with a link to join the session. |
    | --- | --- |
