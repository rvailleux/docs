There are 2 ways to pay for a teleconsultation:

* At the end of the teleconsultation, from the end of session page.
* From the message received once the teleconsultation is over.

| **From the session** | **From the message** |
| --- | --- |
| | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/prerequis.png) | The teleconsultation is over. |
| --- | --- | | | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/prerequis.png) | You received a message inviting you to pay the teleconsultation. |
| --- | --- | |
| - Click&#160;**Pay Now**. <br><br> <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-pay-now.png" alt=""> <br><br>
| - Click on the&#160;**link&#160;**to pay the amount. <br><br> <br><br> <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-make-a-payment-message.png" alt=""> <br><br>
   |

{% hint style="success" %}
You are directed to the payment page.
{% endhint %}

1. Fill in the information.
2. Check the box**I accept the general conditions of sale**.
3. Click **Next**.
 
    .![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-stripe-making-payment-billing-information.png)
4. Enter your credit card information.
5. Click **Next**.
 
    **![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-stripe-making-payment-method.png)**
6. Check the information and click **Yes, I confirm my payment**.
 
    ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-stripe-making-payment-confirmation.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The payment is made.
{% endhint %}
