#  Join the session

{% hint style="info" %}
You received a text message or an email with a link to join the session.
{% endhint %}

1. Click the **link**in the message.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-message-invitation.png)

1. Enter your **name** and **first name** then, click **Confirm**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-with-contact-form.png)
2. Enter the **security code** you just received by message then, click **Confirm**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-with-security-code.png) 

{% hint style="info" %}
If no code is asked to you then, follow the next step.
{% endhint %}
3. Tick the boxes to agree to the terms then, click **Confirm.**

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-session-consent-optin-confirm-click.png)
4. **Allow**your web browser to use the microphone and the camera.

If you do not allow, the participants will not hear you and see you.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-allow-web-browser-use-microphone-camera-zoom.png)
5. Get ready, check your microphone and camera then, click **Join teleconsultation**.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-session-check-peripherals.png)

![](../../.gitbook/assets/apizee-background.png)**Want to change your background?**
{% hint style="info" %}
**See also** [Change the virtual background](../configure-my-teleconsultation-settings/change-the-virtual-background.md)
{% endhint %}
{% hint style="success" %}
You are in the waiting room.
{% endhint %}

Wait for the organizer to allow you to enter into the session. 

The organizer has been informed and they can send you messages to keep you informed. |
| --- | --- |

![](../../.gitbook/assets/FR-conf-rejoindre-guest-salle-attente-message-notif.gif)

{% hint style="success" %}
The communication starts.
{% endhint %}
