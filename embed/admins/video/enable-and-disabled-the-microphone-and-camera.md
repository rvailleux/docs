# How to disable and enable my microphone and camera during a session

{% hint style="info" %}
You are now in a video session.
As a guest: you check your settings before entering the video session.
{% endhint %}

### Enable/Disable Camera

* In the toolbar, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/cameraOn.png)to disable and![](../../.gitbook/assets/CameraOff.png) to enable the camera.

### Enable/Disable Micro

* In the toolbar, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/microphoneOn.png)to disable and ![](../../.gitbook/assets/microphoneOff.png)to enable the micro.

{% hint style="success" %}
You have your video feed to see the changes apply directly.
{% endhint %}
