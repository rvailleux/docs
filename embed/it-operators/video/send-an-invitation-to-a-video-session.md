# Send an invitation message to a video session

{% hint style="info" %}
If Apizee Embed is integrated in a platform, you may have automatically the guest phone number prefilled in our invitation form.
If the phone number is correct, click **Invite and start** to start the video session.
{% endhint %}

1. To add another guest, enter a phone number or an email address in the invitation field.
2. Click **Add**. 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/warning.png) | You can invite only the maximum permitted number of participants to the video session. |
    | --- | --- |
3. When all contact details are entered, click **Invite and start**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The timeline displays. It shows the status of each guest while they join the session.
{% endhint %}
