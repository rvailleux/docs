1. On the right hand-side, click the **cogwheel**to display the calling options. 
 
 ![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/Diag-portal-agent-settings-options.png)
2. Click the drop-down menus to activate and deactivate the tones for the different actions displayed.
3. Click **Save**. 
 
 ![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/EN-Diag-portal-agent-setting-tones.png)
4. Click the drop-down menus to select the microphone and the camera you want to use, the bandwidth and the resolution you need.
5. Click **Save**. 
 
 ![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/EN-Diag-portal-agent-setting-camera-microphone-bandwidth-resolution.png)
6. Tick the box to **enable the notifications** and see them display on the bottom of your screen.
7. Click **Save**. 
 
 ![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/EN-Diag-portal-agent-setting-desktop-notifications.png)

{% hint style="success" %}

The settings are saved.

{% endhint %}
