1. On the right hand-side, click the **Directory** to choose a contact.

![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/EN-portal-transfer-message-click-directory.png)
2. Tick the box in front of the name of the person you want to transfer to conversation to.

![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/EN-portal-transfer-message-choose-contact.png)
3. Write a message to your coworker then, click **Transfer**.

![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/EN-portal-transfer-message-click-transfer.png)

{% hint style="success" %}

The coworker receives a notification and accepts the transfer.<br><br>This message displays once the transfer is accepted: **Another agent is viewing this chat**.

{% endhint %}
