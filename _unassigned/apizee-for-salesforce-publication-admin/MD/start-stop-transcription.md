# How to start or stop the transcription

| ![](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-content-reuse/prerequis.png) | The Admin has enabled transcription in the interactionTemplate configuration.<br><ul></ul> |
| --- | --- |

**To start transcription:**
 
1. During a video call, click the **Transcription** button in the callbar.

| ![](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-content-reuse/ok.png) | Transcription starts : A text panel appears at the bottom left of the screen. The panel shows each speaker's words in real time. |
| --- | --- |

**To stop transcription:**

1. Click the **Transcription** button again.

| ![](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-content-reuse/ok.png) | Transcription stops : The text panel disappears. |
| --- | --- |

| ![Information](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-content-reuse/info.png) | When you start or stop transcription, the change applies to all participants in the call.<br>If the Admin configured transcription to start automatically, the text panel appears as soon as the call begins. You do not need to click the button to start it. |
| --- | --- |

| ![](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-content-reuse/warning.png) | If the Transcription button does not appear in the callbar, transcription is not enabled in your configuration.<br>Contact your Admin.<br><ul></ul> |
| --- | --- |
