# How to configure my settings

| ![](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-content-reuse/prerequis.png) | You are now in a video session.<br>As a guest: you check your settings before entering the video session. |
| --- | --- |

1. During a video session, in the toolbar, click ![](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-appvisio-reuse/configure-my-video-session-settings/configure-my-video-session-settings-2025-03-05.png)to access the configuration menu.
2. Then, for each device (camera and microphone), you can:
    * Activate or desactivate the device.
    * Select the device you want to use.

| ![](../Storage/apizee-for-salesforce-publication-admin/project-content-reuse/ok.png) | You have your video feed to see the changes apply directly. |
