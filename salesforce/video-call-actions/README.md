# How to join a video session as a guest

{% hint style="info" %}
You received a text message.
{% endhint %}

1. In the message, tap the link.

    | ![Information](../.gitbook/assets/info.png) | *The guest app opens in their browser.* |
    | --- | --- |
2. Tick the boxes to Agree to the legal terms.
3. Click **Continue**.
4. Allow the Web browser to use the microphone and camera.
5. Check your settings (camera, microphone).
6. Click **Join session**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The video session page displays.
{% endhint %}

# How to join a video session as an agent

{% hint style="info" %}
You just sent an invitation to a guest to join the video assistance.
{% endhint %}

* After sending the invitation, you will be able to follow the guest throughout their journey in the Apizee application.
* At the end of the guest journey, a devices usage authorisation pop-in appears.
* Allow the Web browser to use the camera and microphone.

{% hint style="success" %}
The video session starts.
{% endhint %}

You can do several actions during this video session :

* [Enable and disabled the microphone and camera](enable-and-disabled-microphone-and-camera.md)
* [Take a picture](take-a-picture.md)
* [Change camera](change-camera.md)
* [Share the pointer](share-pointer.md)
* [Share a screen](share-screen.md)
* [Turn on the guest flashlight](turn-on-guest-flashlight.md)
* [Configure the video settings](configure-video-settings.md)
* [Leave a video session](leave-video-session.md)
* [Start and stop Transcription](start-stop-transcription.md)
