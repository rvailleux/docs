# How to share your screen

{% hint style="info" %}
You are now in a video session.
{% endhint %}

1. In the toolbar, click ![](../.gitbook/assets/screenshare.png)if you want to share your own screen.
2. Choose the screen you want to share.
3. If you want to share a video and you want the participants to hear the video sound, tick the box **Share audio**.
This option may depend on your Web browser.
4. Click **Share**.
![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-share-screen-web-browser.png)
5. To stop the screen sharing, click the ![](../.gitbook/assets/screenshare.png)button again.
