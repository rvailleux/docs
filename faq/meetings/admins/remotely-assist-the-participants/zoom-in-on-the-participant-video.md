# zoom-in-on-the-participant-video

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you cannot see clearly what the participant is showing to you.

You want to zoom in or out on this participant video.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab&#x20;
2. Click the **Guests** tab.

&#x20;3\. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu. 4. In the **Zoom** button, click **+** or **-** according to your need.
