# switch-camera

|   | Available only if you have **several cameras** on your device. |
| - | -------------------------------------------------------------- |

{% hint style="info" %}
You are participating to an ongoing session and you want to switch your camera.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab&#x20;
2. If you are the organizer of the session, click the **Myself** tab.
3. Click **Switch the camera**.
