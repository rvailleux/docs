# invite-new-participant-join-ongoing-video-coneference

{% hint style="info" %}
You are participating to an ongoing session and you want to invite someone to this session.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Participants** tab.
2. Click **Invite**.

&#x20;3\. Enter the contact of the person you want to invite. 4. Enter a personal **message** and click **Send invitation**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The invitation is sent. The person to whom you sent an invitation will receive a message with a link to join the session.
{% endhint %}

You can also invite a person to join the session by phone. Interested? Click **Show more**. \[+] [Show More](https://github.com/rvailleux/docs/tree/master/faq/meetings/admins/actions-during-the-conference/javascript:void\(0\)/README.md) \[-] [Hide](https://github.com/rvailleux/docs/tree/master/faq/meetings/admins/actions-during-the-conference/javascript:void\(0\)/README.md)

1. On the right, in the **Participants** tab, click **More**.

&#x20;2\. Under **Join by phone**, copy the **phone number** and the **DTMF code**.

&#x20;3\. Send it to the participant you want to invite.
