# take-a-picture-and-draw-on-it

{% hint style="info" %}
You are an organizer or a logged user and you want to take a picture of a participant video.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
Drawing on a picture can be **launched by the organizer** of the session only. The guest can [**draw on the picture**](take-a-picture-and-draw-on-it.md#picture-opened-in-whiteboard) only once the whiteboard is opened by the organizer.
{% endhint %}

1. Move your mouse on the participant video and click&#x20;

```
| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The picture is taken. |
| --- | --- |
```

2\. If you want to open the picture in the whiteboard in order to draw on it, click&#x20;

```
| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The picture opens in the whiteboard. |
| --- | --- |
```

&#x20;3\. Choose a **color** and a shape (**pen**, **arrow**, **circle**, **rectangle**) and draw on the picture. You can also annotate it with **Text**.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Save** your work: Click&#x20;
{% endhint %}
