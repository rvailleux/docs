# hide-a-participant-video

{% hint style="info" %}
You are a guest participating to an ongoing session. Someone's video is bothering you and you want to hide it, but just for you.
{% endhint %}

1\. On the top left of the participant video, click !\[]\(../../.gitbook/assets/button-participant-option.png)

&#x20;2\. Click **Turn off video** to hide the participant video, just for you.

```
| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | You do not see anymore the participant. But the other participants still see that person. |
| --- | --- |
```

3\. Click the button again to activate the participant video.
