# join-and-leave-the-conference-as-a-guest

## Join the session

{% hint style="info" %}
You received a text message or an email with a link to join the session.
{% endhint %}

1. Click the **link**in the message.
2. Enter your name then, click **Confirm**.

```
| ![Information](../../.gitbook/assets/info.png) | If you have an Apizee account, click **Log in**. |
| --- | --- |
```

2\. Tick the boxes to agree to the terms then, click **Confirm**.

&#x20;3\. **Allow**your web browser to use the microphone and the camera. If you do not allow, the participants will not hear you and see you.

&#x20;4\. Get ready, check your microphone and camera then, click **Join conference**. If you want it, you can turn off the microphone and the camera before joining the session. You can turn them on again later.
