## How to share your screen

{% hint style="info" %}
You are now in a video session.
{% endhint %}

1. In the toolbar, click if you want to share your own screen.
2. Choose the screen you want to share.
3. If you want to share a video and you want the participants to hear the video sound, tick the box **Share audio**. This option may depend on your Web browser.
4. Click **Share**.&#x20;
5. To stop the screen sharing, click the button again.
