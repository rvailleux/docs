# change-my-password

1. On the top right, click on your **Profile**.
2. Click **My account**..

```
| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The **User account** displays on the **Personal information tab**. |
| --- | --- |
```

3\. Click **Change my password**.
