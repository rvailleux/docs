# join-and-leave-assistance-as-guest

## Join the session

{% hint style="info" %}
You received a text message or an email with a link to join the session.
{% endhint %}

1. Click the **link**in the message.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-message-invitation.png)

1. Enter your name then, click **Confirm**.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-join-session-free-access.png)

```
| ![Information](../.gitbook/assets/info.png) | If you have an Apizee account, click **Log in**. |
| --- | --- |
```

2\. Tick the boxes to agree to the terms then, click **Confirm**.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-join-session-consent-optin-confirm.png) 3. **Allow**your web browser to use the microphone and the camera. If you do not allow, the participants will not hear you and see you.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/EN-allow-web-browser-use-microphone-camera-zoom (1).png>) 4. Get ready, check your microphone and camera then, click **Join conference**. If you want it, you can turn off the microphone and the camera before joining the session. You can turn them on again later.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-join-session-check-peripherals.png)
