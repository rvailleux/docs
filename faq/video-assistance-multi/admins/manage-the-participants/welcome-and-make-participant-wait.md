# welcome-and-make-participant-wait

{% hint style="info" %}
You are an organizer.\
It is time for the appointment and you joined the session.\
The guest is ready to join the session too.
{% endhint %}

1. If you are ready, click **Allow** to let the guest enter and start to communicate with you.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-allow-participant-enter-session.png) 2. If you are not ready: \* Click **Notify waiting room** to inform all the people in the waiting room that you are late. OR \* Click the ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/button-message-bubble.png)(**Notify participant**) to inform only the person which name displays above the button. \* Write your message and click **Send message**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-send-message-participant-before-entering-session.png)
