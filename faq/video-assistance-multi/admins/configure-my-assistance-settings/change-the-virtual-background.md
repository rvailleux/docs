# change-the-virtual-background

1. On the right, click the **Settings** tab.
2. Click ![change background button](../../../.gitbook/assets/background-edit-button.png)to change the background.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-settings-change-background-button.png) 3. Choose a background or a blurred effect. 4. If you want to customize and upload a new background, click **Add**. 5. Choose an image and click **Open**. 6. Click **Confirm**.

![choose background or effect](../../../.gitbook/assets/izeeconf-backgroud-features.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The background or the blurred effect displays.
{% endhint %}

![background or blurred effect in izeeconf](../../../.gitbook/assets/conf-background-blur-effect.gif)
