# remote-assist-open-shared-files-library

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to help a participant to open the library of the files shared during the session.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/button-actions.png)
2. Click the **Guests** tab.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-actions-guests-tab-selected.png) 3. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu. 4. Click **Files**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-menu-action-orga-guests-tab-files.png)

```
| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | An invitation is sent to the participant and displays on his screen as follow: |
| --- | --- |
```
