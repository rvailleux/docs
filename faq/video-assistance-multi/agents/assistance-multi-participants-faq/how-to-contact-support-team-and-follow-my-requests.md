# how-to-contact-support-team-and-follow-my-requests

1.  Go to the [Apizee Support Center](https://apizee.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portals).

    |   | You are directed to the Apizee Support Website. |
    | - | ----------------------------------------------- |

## Create an Apizee support account

1. To create a free account, click **Sign up**.

&#x20;2\. Enter your email address the, click **Send link**.

&#x20;3\. From your mailbox, open and click the link. 4. Enter your last name and first name, choose a password and click **Sign up**.

{% hint style="success" %}
You are logged in to your Apizee Support account.
{% endhint %}

## Create a support request

1. To create a request, click **Apizee Technical Support**.

&#x20;2\. Choose the **product** you use.

&#x20;3\. Enter a **summary** and a **description**. 4. If needed, add an attached file. 5. Click **Send**.​

{% hint style="success" %}
The overview of your request displays.
{% endhint %}

## Follow a support request

1. To follow your support request, please log in to your Apizee Support account.
   1. Enter your email address then, click **Next**.

&#x20;2\. Enter your password then, click **Log in**.

&#x20;2\. At the top right, click **Requests – Created by me**.

&#x20;3\. Find the request you want then, click it.

{% hint style="success" %}
The overview of your request displays.
{% endhint %}
