# follow-a-ticket

1. In the left-hand menu, click the service you want.
2. In the ticket list, find the ticket you want to follow up and click&#x20;

{% hint style="success" %}
The page of the ticket displays. You can find the following information:
{% endhint %}

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-assistance-multi-portal-diag-assistance-page-overview.png)

\| a. | Ticket request | Information given when creating the ticket. | To edit the ticket information:

* Click in the fields to change the content then  - Click **Save**. | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | b. | When | Date and time set for the assistance. | If the scheduling option is activated, you can change an immediate appointment into a scheduled one and vice versa.
* Click **Immediate** or **Scheduled**.- Click **Save**.- Click **Invite again**.- Follow the steps on the screen to send a new invitation for the same assistance to your guest.

\|  | The guest receives a message with the new information. | | --- | --- | | | c. | Assignement | - Person in charge of the ticket. - Assistance report. | - Click the **Assign to** drop-down menu to assign the ticket to another person. - Click **Choose File** to upload the assistance report.

* Files opened in the whiteboard during the session - Files sent to the agent by the requester before or after the session - Pictures the agent took remotely - PDF of the session information if exported.
