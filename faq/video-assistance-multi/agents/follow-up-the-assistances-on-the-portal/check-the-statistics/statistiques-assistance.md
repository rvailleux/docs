# statistiques-assistance

This **Statistics - Assistance** page lists all the assistance sessions/tickets made within your company or structure.

Easily analyze the assistance sessions thanks to the key figures and the diagrams.

1. On the left-hand menu, under **Supervision**, click **Statistics**then, click **Assistance dashboard**.

```
| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The statistic page displays |
| --- | --- |
```

2\. Click the calendar drop-down menu (a) to display the statistics for a given period.

```
| ![Information](../../../.gitbook/assets/info.png) | The ticket data is updated every 24 hours.
```

The current day information is available the next day. | | --- | --- | 3. Choose to display the result for a specific **service** (b). 4. Choose an **organizer** (the person who ran the session) or a user group in the drop-down menu (c) to display the statistics of user or a group.

&#x20;5\. Click Display (d). 6. Click **Export** (e) to export the result of the search in csv format.
