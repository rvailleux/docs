# hide-a-participant-video-organizer

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to turn off the video of participant, but just for you.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/button-actions.png)
2. Click the **Guests** tab.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-actions-guests-tab-selected.png) 3. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu. 4. Click **Turn off the participant video**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-menu-action-orga-guests-tab-turn-off-participant-video.png)

```
| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The participant video is cut just for the organizer. The other participants still see that person. |
| --- | --- |
```

5\. Click the button again to activate the participant video.
