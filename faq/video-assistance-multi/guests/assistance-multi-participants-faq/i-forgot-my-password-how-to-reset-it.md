# i-forgot-my-password-how-to-reset-it

{% hint style="info" %}
You want to log in to your account but you forgot your password.
{% endhint %}

1. On the log in page, click **Password forgotten?**&#x20;
2. Enter your login.
3. Tick the box **I'm not a robot**.
4.  Click **Reset**.

    |   | An email is sent with a reset password link. |
    | - | -------------------------------------------- |
5. Click the link in the email.
6. Choose a new password and enter it again to confirm it.

Mix up the characters: 12 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 digit, 1 special character.

Protect your password: Keep it in memory, change it regularly and do not save it in a file or on a piece of paper. | | --- | --- | 7. Click **Save**.

{% hint style="success" %}
Your password is changed, you are logged in to your account.
{% endhint %}
