# Send an invitation to a video assistance

## From the Contact tab

1. In the list, click the name of the contact.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/send-an-invitation-2025-03-07 (1).png>) 2. In Apizee widget, fill in the contact's mobile phone number. 3. Click **Invite**.

| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The invitation is sent by text message. The guest receives a link to join the video assistance. |
| ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/tip.png) | If the contact already has a mobile phone number completed in their informations, the phone number field will be automatically pre-filled. |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
