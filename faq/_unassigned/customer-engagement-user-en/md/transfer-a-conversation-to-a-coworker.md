# transfer-a-conversation-to-a-coworker

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/prerequis (1).png>) | A visitor sent a message to you and you want to transfer the conversation to a coworker. |
| ------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

1. On the right hand-side, click the **Directory** to choose a contact.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-transfer-message-click-directory.png) 2. Tick the box in front of the name of the person you want to transfer to conversation to.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-transfer-message-choose-contact.png) 3. Write a message to your coworker then, click **Transfer**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-transfer-message-click-transfer.png)

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/ok (1).png>) | <p>The coworker receives a notification and accepts the transfer.<br><br>This message displays once the transfer is accepted: <strong>Another agent is viewing this chat</strong>.</p> |
| ------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
