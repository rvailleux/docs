# configure-my-chat-box-settings

1. On the right hand-side, click the **cogwheel**to display the calling options.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-portal-agent-settings-options (1).png>) 2. Click the drop-down menus to activate and deactivate the tones for the different actions displayed. 3. Click **Save**.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-portal-agent-setting-tones (1).png>) 4. Click the drop-down menus to select the microphone and the camera you want to use, the bandwidth and the resolution you need. 5. Click **Save**.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-portal-agent-setting-camera-microphone-bandwidth-resolution (1).png>) 6. Tick the box to **enable the notifications** and see them display on the bottom of your screen. 7. Click **Save**.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-portal-agent-setting-desktop-notifications (1).png>)

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/ok (1).png>) | The settings are saved. |
