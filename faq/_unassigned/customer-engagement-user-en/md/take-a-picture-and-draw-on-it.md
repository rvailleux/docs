# take-a-picture-and-draw-on-it

1. To take a picture of the participant video, click ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/Button-take-picture.png)
2. Click **Local annotation** to edit the picture without being seen by the participant.
3. Click **Shared annotation** to edit the picture and let the interlocutor see what you do at the same time.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-portal-agent-local-shared-annotation-open-whiteboard.png)

```
| ![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/project-content-reuse/ok.png) | The picture displays in the whiteboard. |
| --- | --- |
```

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-portal-agent-whiteboard.png) 4. Choose a **color**and a shape (**pen**, **arrow**, **circle**, **rectangle**) and draw on the picture. You can also annotate it with **Text**.

```
| ![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/project-content-reuse/tip.png) | Do not forget to **Save** your work. Click ![](../Storage/customer-engagement-user-en/project-content-reuse/button-save.png) |
| --- | --- |
```

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-whiteboard-options.png)

| a.      | Move around the picture                                                                                                                        |
| ------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| b.      | Draw                                                                                                                                           |
| c.      | Colors and stroke size                                                                                                                         |
| d.      | Shapes, text and eraser                                                                                                                        |
| e.      | Delete the drawings, delete the picture, upload a new picture, rotate, take a picture with my camera, take a picture of the interlocutor video |
| f. & g. | Cancel and Restore                                                                                                                             |
| h. & i  | Zoom in and Zoom out                                                                                                                           |
