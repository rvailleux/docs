# Deactivate the camera of all the participants

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to turn off everyone's camera.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab&#x20;
2. Click the **Organizer** tab.

&#x20;3\. If you want to cut all the participants cameras, click **Turn off all videos**.

```
| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The camera of all the participants are turned off. The organizer is the only one that can be seen. |
| --- | --- |
```

4\. Click the button again to activate the all the cameras.
