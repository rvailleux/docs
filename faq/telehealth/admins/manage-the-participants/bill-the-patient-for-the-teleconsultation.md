# bill-the-patient-for-the-teleconsultation

{% hint style="info" %}
This is the end of the teleconsultation & You want to charge the teleconsultation.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, in the **Participants**tab, click **€ Bill**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-bill-patient-button.png) 2. Choose or enter the amount to be paid. Minimum charge 1€. 3. Choose the receiving account of the payment in the drop-down menu.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-bill-patient-choose-receiving-account.png) 4. Click **Send**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The patient receives a message with a link to proceed to the payment.
{% endhint %}

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-make-a-payment-message.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The following window displays when the patient leaves the teleconsultation, so that, the patient can directly pay for the teleconsultation.
{% endhint %}

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-pay-now.png)
