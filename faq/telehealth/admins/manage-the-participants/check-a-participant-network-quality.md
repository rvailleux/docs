# check-a-participant-network-quality

{% hint style="info" %}
You want to check the quality state of a participant video or audio.
{% endhint %}

##

On the top left of the participant video, click the **network**symbol.

{% hint style="success" %}
For example: If the video quality is bad, you can ask the participant to turn off the camera. That could improve the quality of the video session.
{% endhint %}
