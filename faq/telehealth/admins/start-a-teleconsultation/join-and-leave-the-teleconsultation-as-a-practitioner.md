# join-and-leave-the-teleconsultation-as-a-practitioner

## Join the session

{% hint style="info" %}
You are logged in to your account.
{% endhint %}

1. In the left-hand menu, click the service you want.
2. In the **List**, find the session you want to join then, click **Join**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-teleconsultation-list-view-join-session.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The window opens.
{% endhint %}

1. **Allow**your web browser to use the microphone and the camera. If you do not allow, the participants will not hear you and see you.
2. Get ready, check your microphone and camera. You will be able to turn off the microphone and the camera during the session.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-session-practitioner-check-peripherals.png)

**Want to change your background?**

| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

{% hint style="success" %}
Wait for the guest to connect to the session.
{% endhint %}
