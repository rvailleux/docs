# join-and-leave-the-teleconsultation-as-a-patient

## Join the session

{% hint style="info" %}
You received a text message or an email with a link to join the session.
{% endhint %}

1. Click the **link**in the message.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-message-invitation.png)

1. Enter your **name** and **first name** then, click **Confirm**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-with-contact-form.png) 2. Enter the **security code** you just received by message then, click **Confirm**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-with-security-code.png)

```
| ![Information](../../.gitbook/assets/info.png) | If no code is asked to you then, follow the next step. |
| --- | --- |
```

3\. Tick the boxes to agree to the terms then, click **Confirm.**

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-session-consent-optin-confirm-click.png) 4. **Allow**your web browser to use the microphone and the camera.

If you do not allow, the participants will not hear you and see you.

&#x20;5\. Get ready, check your microphone and camera then, click **Join teleconsultation**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-teleconsultation-join-session-check-peripherals.png)

**Want to change your background?**

|   | **See also** [Change the virtual background](../configure-my-teleconsultation-settings/change-the-virtual-background.md) |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

{% hint style="success" %}
You are in the waiting room.
{% endhint %}

Wait for the organizer to allow you to enter into the session.

The organizer has been informed and he/she can send you messages to keep you informed. | | --- | --- |

{% hint style="success" %}
The communication starts.
{% endhint %}
