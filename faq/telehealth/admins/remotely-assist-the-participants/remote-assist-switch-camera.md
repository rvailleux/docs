# remote-assist-switch-camera

|   | Available only if the participant has **several cameras** on his device. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to help a participant to change his camera.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab&#x20;
2. Click the **Guests** tab.

&#x20;3\. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu. 4. Click **Switch the camera**.
