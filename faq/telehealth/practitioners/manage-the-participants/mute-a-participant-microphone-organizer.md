# mute-a-participant-microphone-organizer

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to mute a participant in particular.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab&#x20;
2. Click the **Guests** tab.

&#x20;3\. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu. 4. Click **Mute the participant**.

```
| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The participant microphone is deactivated. Nobody can hear the participant. |
| --- | --- |
```

5\. Click the button again to unmute and activate the participant microphone.
