# send-a-quick-invitation

1. In the menu bar, at the top right, click **Quick invitation**.&#x20;
2. In the drop-down menu, choose the service (flow) with which you want to send the invitation.
3. Enter the participants email and/or phone numbers.&#x20;
4. Click **Send invitation**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The invitation is sent. The participants will receive a message with a link to join the video session. The meeting page displays on the agent screen to directly join the meeting.
{% endhint %}
