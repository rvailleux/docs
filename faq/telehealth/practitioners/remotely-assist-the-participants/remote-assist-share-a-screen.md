|   | Available if the participant is following the session with a **computer only**. |
| - | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to help a participant to share his screen.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab&#x20;
2. Click the **Guests** tab.

&#x20;3\. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu. 4. Click **Share screen**.
