# activate-the-online-billing

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/Apizee-credit-card-drawing.png)

Activate the online billing in order to charge a patient for the teleconsultation. You need first to activate the feature from the Apizee portal. Then, choose whether you want transfers to the business account of your structure or to an individual account.

Want both? Of course, it is possible!

* [Configure the individual billing](configure-the-individual-billing.md)

{% hint style="info" %}
We use [Stripe](https://stripe.com/), as a securized payment platform. It allows you to make online billings. We do not rely on Stripe potential updates. That is why you may find some slight differences between the following procedures and what you see on your screen.
{% endhint %}
