# configure-the-individual-billing

{% hint style="success" %}
The user needs to configure a **Stripe** profile and fill in the bank account details from his/her **own** user portail to enjoy the individual payments without using the business or structure account.
{% endhint %}

## Creating a Stripe profile

{% hint style="info" %}
Stripe is a securized payment platform. Stripe needs information to ensure the transfers between the patient account and the business account.
{% endhint %}

1. On the top right, click on your **Profile**.
2. Click **My account**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-practitioner-thombnail-profile.png) 3. Click on the **Billing** tab. 4. Under **Transfer to an individual account**, click **Create a Stripe profile**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-activating-online-billing-user-account-billing-tab-create-stripe-profile.png)

```
| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | You are being directed to **Stripe** Website. |
| --- | --- |
```

### Personal information

***

1. Fill in your personal information.
2. Click **Next**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-stripe-individual-online-billing-personal-information.png)

### Business details

***

1. Choose your business **industry** in the drop-down menu.
2. If you do not have a **W\*\*\*\*ebsite**, click **Add a product description instead**.
3. Enter the medical specialty (activity) + the indication “Teleconsultation”.
4. Click **Next**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-stripe-individual-online-billing-business-details.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The information is being verified
{% endhint %}

Stripe can randomly ask for more information to verify the identity of the account representative.

## Adding a bank account

{% hint style="info" %}
You configured a Stripe profile.\
You are logged in to your account, on the **User account** page, on the **Billing** tab.
{% endhint %}

1. Click **Add a new bank account**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-activate-individual-online-billing-add-bank-account.png) 2. Enter the **holder name** of the bank account. 3. Enter the **international IBAN number** of the bank account. 4. Click **Continue**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-activate-individual-online-billing-bank-account-IBAN.png) 5. Enter a **Pre-defined amount**. The amounts will display during the online billing as a quick entry. You will be able to choose between the different amounts or enter a new one. Minimum charge: 1€. 6. Click **Save**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-activate-individual-online-billing-predefined-amount.png)

The pre-defined amounts display as follow during the online billing:
