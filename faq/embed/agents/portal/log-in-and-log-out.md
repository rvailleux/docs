# Log in

{% hint style="info" %}
You have the URL and you clicked it.
The log in page displays.
{% endhint %}

1. Enter your **email** address
2. Enter your **password** and
3. Click **Sign In**.

Or sign in with an external account, if this option is available.

1. Under **Or sign in with**, click the button according to the external account you want to log in with.

{% hint style="success" %}
The page loads and your are logged in.
{% endhint %}

# Log out

{% hint style="info" %}
You are logged in.
{% endhint %}

1. On the upper right side, click **Log out**.

{% hint style="success" %}
You are directed to the log in page.
{% endhint %}
