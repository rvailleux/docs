# Get all video calls attached to a Case or an Incident

{% hint style="info" %}
* The Apizee Visual Support app is installed and activated on your ServiceNow instance.
 * You use the CSM or ITSM Workspace (**classicUI**)
{% endhint %}

# 

Each time you start a video call from a Case or an Incident details page, an "Apizee Session" entry is created and attached to the Case or incident.

1. Open the Case or Incident details page.

![](../.gitbook/assets/Frame 10 (1).png)
2. Scroll down and click on the "Apizee Sessions" tab in the lower part of the page.

![](../.gitbook/assets/Frame 12.png)
