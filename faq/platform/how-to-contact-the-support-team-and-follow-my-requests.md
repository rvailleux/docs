# Create an Apizee support account

1. To create a free account, click **Sign up**.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-create-new-account-click-sign-up.png)
2. Enter your email address the, click **Send link**.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-send-sign-up-link.png)
3. From your mailbox, open and click the link.
4. Enter your last name and first name, choose a password and click **Sign up**.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-enter-name-choose-password.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
You are logged in to your Apizee Support account.
{% endhint %}

# Create a support request

1. To create a request, click **Apizee Technical Support**.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-click-Technical-support.png)
2. Choose the **product** you use.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-click-to-choose-product.png)
3. Enter a **summary** and a **description**.
4. If needed, add an attached file.
5. Click **Send**.​

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-request-description-click-send.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The overview of your request displays.
{% endhint %}

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-preview-support-request.png)

# Follow a support request

1. To follow your support request, please log in to your Apizee Support account.
    1. Enter your email address then, click **Next**.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-log-in-to-support-center-click-next.png)
    2. Enter your password then, click **Log in**.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-log-in-to-support-center-password-click-log-in.png)
2. At the top right, click **Requests – Created by me**.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-click-requests-created-by-me.png)
3. Find the request you want then, click it.

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-requests-list.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The overview of your request displays.
{% endhint %}

![](../.gitbook/assets/EN-Apizee-support-center-preview-support-request.png)
