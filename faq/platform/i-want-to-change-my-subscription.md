**You subscribed to an offer and you want to change it?**

1. Log in to your Apizee portal.
2. On the left-hand menu, click **Company** then, click **Subscriptions**.
3. Click on your current subscription product.
4. Change it.

**You want to downgrade your subscription?**

Please, contact our [Support Team](https://apizee.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portals). Or, if you are followed by an Apizee contact, please, contact him/her.
