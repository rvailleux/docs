# choose-my-portal-dashboard

1. At the top right, click on your **Profile**.
2. Click **My account**.

&#x20;3\. Click on the **Settings**tab. 4. Under **Choose the dashboard**, in the drop-down menu, choose the dashboard you want. 5. Click **Save**.
