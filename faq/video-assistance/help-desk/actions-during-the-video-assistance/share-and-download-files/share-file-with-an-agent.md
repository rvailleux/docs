# share-file-with-an-agent

## Before a call

You can send files to an agent even if the agent is unavailable.

1. Click **Upload media** to transfer files.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-guest-share-file-before-session-click-upload-media-f3074fa7.png) 2. Click **Upload media** and choose the documents you want to share.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-guest-share-file-before-session-click-upload-media2.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The agent receives the documents. If you sent a picture, you can see a preview.
{% endhint %}

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-guest-share-file-before-session-uploaded-files-preview.png)

## During the assistance call

1. If you want to send a picture to the agent with your mobile camera, click ![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/button-camera-plus.png)

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-requester-mobile-take-picture-with-mobile-camera.png)

```
| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | Your mobile phone camera opens. |
| --- | --- |
```

2\. Take a picture then, click **OK**. 3. Allow to share your geolocation.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-guest-allow-geolocation-f614036d.png)

```
| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The file charges and displays in the **Messages** tab. |
| --- | --- |
```

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-requester-mobile-take-picture-displays-messages-tab.png)

## After a call

1. From the assistance page, click **Share media**.

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-guest-share-file-after-session-click-share-media-950f984a.png) 2. Choose your document and click **Open**.

```
| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The document charges. |
| --- | --- |

| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The document displays in the messages. |
| --- | --- |
```

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-guest-share-file-after-session-result.png)

{% hint style="info" %}
You can keep sending files to the agent as long as the ticket is open.
{% endhint %}
