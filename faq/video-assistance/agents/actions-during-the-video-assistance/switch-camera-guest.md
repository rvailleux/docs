# How to switch my camera during a video assistance?

| ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/icon-device-several-cameras.png) | Available only if the participant has **several cameras** on his device. |
| ------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |

{% hint style="info" %}
You are participating to an ongoing session as a guest and you want to switch your camera.
{% endhint %}

1. Tap one time on your video.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-guest-tap-video-stream.png) 2. Tap the **switch camera** button ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/switch-camera-button.png)

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-guest-tap-switch-camera-button.png) 3. Tap again until the right camera displays.

{% hint style="success" %}
Need to switch camera to display the [flashight feature](turn-on-my-flashlight.md)? Tap the **switch camera** button until the **flashlight** \*\*\*\* button displays in the upper left corner of the screen.
{% endhint %}

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-guest-tap-switch-camera-display-light-button.png)
