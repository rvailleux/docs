## Change the invitation time?

1. In the left-hand menu, click the service you want.
2. In the ticket list, find the ticket you want and click ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/button-edit.png)

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-service-assistance-edit-ticket.png) 3. Scroll down the page and click **Edit**. 4. Choose another **date** and **time**, if needed, then click **Next**. 5. Choose a message **template** and a **language**,\*\*\*\*then click **Send**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-assistance-page-edit-button.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The guest receives a new invitation to the same session.
{% endhint %}
