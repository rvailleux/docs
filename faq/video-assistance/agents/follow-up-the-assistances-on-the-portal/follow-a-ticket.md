# follow-a-ticket

1. In the left-hand menu, click the service you want.
2. In the ticket list, find the ticket you want to follow up and click ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/button-edit.png)

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-service-assistance-edit-ticket.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The page of the ticket displays. You can find the following information:
{% endhint %}

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-diag-assistance-page-overview.png)

\| a. | Ticket request | Information given when creating the ticket. | To edit the ticket information:

* Click in the fields to change the content then  - Click **Save**.\
  To edit the guest contact:
* Click **Edit this contact** - Change the information you want then, - Click **Save**. ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-portal-ticket-page-edit-requester-information.png) | | --- | --- | --- | --- | | b. | When | Date and time set for the assistance. | If the scheduling option is activated, you can change an immediate appointment into a scheduled one and vice versa.
* Click **Immediate** or **Scheduled**.- Click **Save**.- Click **Invite again**.- Follow the steps on the screen to send a new invitation for the same assistance to your guest.

\| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/ok (2).png>) | The guest receives a message with the new information. | | --- | --- | | | c. | Assignement | - Person in charge of the ticket. - Assistance report. | - Click the **Assign to** drop-down menu to assign the ticket to another person. - Click **Choose File** to upload the assistance report.

\| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Apizee-see-also-icon (1).png>) | **See also** [Attach a report to the ticket](attach-a-report-to-the-ticket.md) | | --- | --- | | | d. | Shared files | Files shared during the assistance.

* Files opened in the whiteboard during the session - Files sent to the agent by the requester before or after the session - Pictures the agent took remotely - PDF of the session information if exported.

| ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/Apizee-see-also-icon (1).png>) | \*\*See also \*\* [Export the session information into a PDF](export-session-information-into-pdf.md)  |
