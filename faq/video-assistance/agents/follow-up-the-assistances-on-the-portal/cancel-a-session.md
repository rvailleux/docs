# Need to cancel a scheduled assistance?

1. In the left-hand menu, click the service you want.
2. In the ticket list, find the ticket you want and click **Cancel**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-service-assistance-list-cancel-session.png) 3. You are going to advise the guest that was invited to the video assistance:

```
1. Choose the message **template** you want, and choose the **language**.
2. Click **Next**.
```

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-diag-agent-cancel-session-choose-template-and-language.png) 3. Add a personnal information that will be added to the message. 4. Click **Send**. ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-diag-agent-cancel-session-add-message-and-send.png) 4. A message displays, click **Cancel session**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/cancel-session-confirmation.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The session is canceled and the guest is informed of the situation.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="success" %}
You can also cancel a session from the assistance page.

* Scroll down the page.- Click **Cancel session**.- Follow the same steps as below.
{% endhint %}

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-assistance-page-cancel-session-button.png)
