# create-a-ticket-quick-invitation-by-email-and-or-sms

1. In the menu bar, at the top right, click **Quick invitation**. ![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-portal-Quick-invitation-click.png)
2. In the drop-down menu, choose the video assistance service (flow) with which you want to send the invitation.
3.  Enter the guest email, phone number or a name if you use the **Directory**.

    | ![Information](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/info (2).png>) | You can invite only 1 person for a video assistance. |
    | ---------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |

![](../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Portal-Quick-invitation-home-page-assistance-1-1.png) 3. Click **Send invitation**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The invitation is sent.

The guest will receive a message with a link to join the video assistance.

The assistance page displays on the agent screen.
{% endhint %}
