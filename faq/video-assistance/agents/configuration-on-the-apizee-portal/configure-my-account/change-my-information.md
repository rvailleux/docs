# change-my-information

1. On the top right, click on your **Profile**.
2.  Click **My account**. ![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-user-thombnail-profile (1).png>)

    | ![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/ok (2).png>) | The **User account** displays on the **Personal information** tab. |
    | --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
3. Change your information: **email**, **names**, **title** (your role in the company) and the **phone numbers**.
4. Click **Save**.

![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-user-account-change-information (1).png>)

{% hint style="success" %}
The new information is saved.
{% endhint %}
