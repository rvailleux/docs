# change-my-language

1. On the top right, click on your **Profile**.
2. Click **My account**.

![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-user-thombnail-profile (1).png>) 3. Under **Language**, click the language you want in the drop-down menu. 4. Click **Save**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The page reloads with the selected language.
{% endhint %}
