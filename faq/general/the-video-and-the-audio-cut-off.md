# Video or audio keeps cutting out

![](../.gitbook/assets/FAQ-video-and-audio-cut-off.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
A bad network connection can influence the quality of the audio and the video.
{% endhint %}

1. From the call bar, click ![](../.gitbook/assets/button-network-quality.png)

![](../.gitbook/assets/call-bar-network-quality.png)
2. If the connection is bad, follow the steps below:

    | You are on a mobile phone |  | You are on a computer |
    | --- | :---: | --- |
    | Activate the WiFi on your mobile phone and continue the conference as long as the WiFi network is stable. | ![](../.gitbook/assets/picto-network-blue.png) | Connect the computer to the modem with an Ethernet cable. |
    | Still having an issue? | ![](../.gitbook/assets/picto-help-blue.png) | Still having an issue? |
    | Deactivate the camera and continue with the audio only. | ![](../.gitbook/assets/picto-camera-blue.png) | Deactivate the camera and continue with the audio only. |
