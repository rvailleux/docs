1. On your mobile phone, copy the link of the session.
2. On your computer, write the link in the search bar of your browser.
3. Click **Join conference**.

**Or**

1. Ask the organizer for another invitation on your email address.
2. Click on the link in the email.
3. Click **Join conference**.
