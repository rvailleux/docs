1. On the top right, click on your **Profile**.
2. Click **My account**.. 
  
 ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-user-thombnail-profile.png)

    | ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The **User account** displays on the **Personal information tab**. |
    | --- | --- |
3. Click **Change my password**. 
 
 ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-user-account-change-password.png) 
 [+] [Show More](javascript:void%280%29)
 [-] [Hide](javascript:void%280%29)
 | ![](../../../.gitbook/assets/tip.png) | Choose a **different password** that you do not use for another Website.

Mix up the characters: 12 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 digit.

Protect your password: Keep it in memory, change it regularly and do not save it in a file or on a piece of paper. |
    | --- | --- |
4. Enter the **old password** and write the **new** one.
5. Click **Save**.

{% hint style="success" %}
The new password is created.
