{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to mute a participant in particular.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-actions.png)
2. Click the **Guests** tab. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-actions-guests-tab-selected.png)
3. If you are more than 2 participants, choose the name of the participant in the drop-down menu.
4. Click **Mute the participant**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-menu-action-orga-guests-tab-mute-participant.png) 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The participant microphone is deactivated. Nobody can hear the participant. |
    | --- | --- |
5. Click the button again to unmute and activate the participant microphone.
