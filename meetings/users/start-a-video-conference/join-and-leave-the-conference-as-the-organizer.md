#  Join the session

{% hint style="info" %}
You are logged in to your account.
{% endhint %}

1. In the left-hand menu, click the service you want.
2. In the **List**, find the session you want to join then, click **Join**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-conf-list-view-join-session.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The window opens.
{% endhint %}

1. **Allow**your web browser to use the microphone and the camera. 
If you do not allow, the participants will not hear you and see you. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-allow-web-browser-use-microphone-camera-zoom.png)

1. Get ready, check your microphone and camera then, click **Join conference**. 
If you want it, you can turn off the microphone and the camera before joining the session. You can turn them on again later. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-join-session-check-peripherals-orga.png) 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/apizee-background.png)**Want to change your background?**

{% hint style="info" %}

**See also** [Change the virtual background](../configure-my-video-conference-settings/change-the-virtual-background.md)

{% endhint %}
{% hint style="success" %}
Wait for the guest to connect to the session.
{% endhint %}
