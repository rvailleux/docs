# How to share a pointer during a video session

{% hint style="info" %}
You are now in a video session.
{% endhint %}

1. In the toolbar, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/share-pointer-2025-03-05.png)to activate a red laser.
2. Hold left click and move the cursor to draw a red line on the remote video feed.

{% hint style="success" %}
*The guest can see what you draw with the pointer on their screen.*
{% endhint %}
