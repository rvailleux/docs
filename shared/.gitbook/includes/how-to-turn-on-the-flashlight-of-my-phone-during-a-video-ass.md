# How to turn on the flashlight of my phone during a video assistance?

| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/icon-device-mobile.png) | Available only if you are following the session with: <br> - an **Android smartphone**. - the **<a href="switch-camera-guest.md" target="_blank">back camera</a>**<a href="switch-camera-guest.md" target="_blank"> selected</a>. - on **Chrome** web browser. |
| --- | --- |

{% hint style="info" %}
The organizer of the session cannot see clearly what you are showing with your camera. 
You want to turn on the flashlight on the back of your phone.
{% endhint %}

1. Tap on the **flashlight**  **button** in the upper left corner of the screen.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/Diag-guest-turn-on-light.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The flash on the back of your mobile phone is on.
{% endhint %}
2. To turn the light off, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/light-button.png)
