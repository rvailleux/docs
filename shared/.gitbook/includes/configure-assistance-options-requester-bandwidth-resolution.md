{% hint style="info" %}
You are a requester for an assistance and you are already in an ongoing session. 
Even if the solution optimizes automatically the bandwidth and the resolution, you may adjust sometimes the picture and video resolution and the bandwidth to fit your needs.
{% endhint %}

1. On the top right, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-display-menu.png)to display the menu.
2. Click the **settings**tab.
3. Click **Network**.
4. Choose in the drop-down menus the **video bandwidth**, the **picture resolution** and the **video resolution** you want to use for the session.
5. Click **Apply**.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-diag-guest-bandwidth-resolution-settings.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The session reloads with the new settings you applied.
{% endhint %}
