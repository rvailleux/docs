You want to configure your peripherals. |
| --- | --- |

1. On the top right, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-display-menu.png)to display the menu.
2. Click the **settings** tab.
3. Choose in the drop-down menus the **camera**, the **microphone** and the **audio** item you want to use for the session
4. Click **Apply**.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-Diag-guest-audio-video-settings.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The session reloads with the new settings you applied.
{% endhint %}
