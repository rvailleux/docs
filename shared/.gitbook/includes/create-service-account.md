1. Select the **Information** tab.
2. Click **Create** under **Service Account**.
3. In the **New Service Account** pop-up:

    1. Click the **Copy** icon next to the **Client ID**.
    2. Save the **Client ID** in your integration.
    3. Click the **Copy** icon next to the **Client secret**.
    4. Save the **Client secret** in your integration.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Keep the client secret safe.
 The client secret is not available after this step.
{% endhint %}
