{% hint style="info" %}
You are an organizer and for some reason, you need to kick a participant out of the session.
{% endhint %}

1. On the top left of the participant video, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-participant-option.png) 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/conf-participant-option-dots-click.png)
2. At the bottom of the list, click **Remove from conference**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-dots-participant-option-organizer-view.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The participant is out. You can keep running the session with the other participants.
{% endhint %}
