# How to configure my settings

{% hint style="info" %}
You are now in a video session.
As a guest: you check your settings before entering the video session.
{% endhint %}

1. During a video session, in the toolbar, click ![](../../.gitbook/assets/configure-my-video-session-settings-2025-03-05.png)to access the configuration menu.
2. Then, for each device (camera and microphone), you can:
    * Activate or desactivate the device.
    * Select the device you want to use.

{% hint style="success" %}
You have your video feed to see the changes apply directly.
{% endhint %}
