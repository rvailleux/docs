**You have an appointment and you want to check your devices before the session starts?**

![](../../.gitbook/assets/FAQ-check-camera-microphone-before-appointment.png)

1. Click the link in the invitation message you received.

{% hint style="success" %}
The video conference page opens.
{% endhint %}
2. Enter your **name**.
3. **Allow** the web browser to use your microphone and camera.
If you do not allow the access, you will not be able to check if they work properly.

![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-allow-web-browser-use-microphone-camera-zoom.png)
4. Check your microphone and camera.
5. When you are ready, click **Join**!
