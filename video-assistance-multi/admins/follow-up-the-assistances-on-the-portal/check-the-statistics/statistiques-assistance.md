This **Statistics - Assistance** page lists all the assistance sessions/tickets made within your company or structure.

Easily analyze the assistance sessions thanks to the key figures and the diagrams.

1. On the left-hand menu, under **Supervision**, click **Statistics**then, click **Assistance dashboard**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-menu-stats-assistance.png)

{% hint style="success" %}
The statistic page displays
{% endhint %}
2. Click the calendar drop-down menu (a) to display the statistics for a given period.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN_see_ticket_statistics_calendar.png)

    | ![Information](../../../.gitbook/assets/info.png) | The ticket data is updated every 24 hours.
The current day information is available the next day. |
    | --- | --- |
3. Choose to display the result for a specific **service** (b).
4. Choose an **organizer** (the person who ran the session) or a user group in the drop-down menu (c) to display the statistics of user or a group.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN_see_ticket_statistics_user.png)
5. Click Display (d).
6. Click **Export** (e) to export the result of the search in csv format.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN_see_assistance_statistics.png)

| a. | Choose the period for which you want to see the statistics. |
| --- | --- |
| b. | Displays the result for a specific service.
The entries in the **Service**drop-down menu depend on the company configuration. They are the same entries as the ones on the left-hand menu, under **Services**.

They are the entries you configured in **Configuration**&gt; **Services management**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-menu-service.png) |
| c. | Displays the result for a group of users, all the organizers, or one in particular.
The organizer is the one that runs the session. |
| d. | Apply and display the selection. |
| e. | Export the data as a CSV. If you want retrieve the [timeline events](../video-assistance/agents/follow-up-the-assistances-on-the-portal/what-is-timeline-for.md) in the export, check the box and click **Export**.

![](../../../.gitbook/assets/EN-portal-assistance-dashboard-export-timeline-in-csv.png) |
| f. | General information about the tickets, the calls, their duration and the files shared during the assistance. |
| g. | Tickets distribution by user group. |
| h. | Tickets distribution by user. |
| i. | Tickets status. |
