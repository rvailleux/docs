{% hint style="info" %}
You are an organizer and you want to record the ongoing session.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-actions.png)
2. Click the **Organizer** tab. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-actions-organizer-tab-selected.png)
3. If you want to record the videos, click **Record video**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-menu-action-organizer-record-video.png)
4. To choose what videos you want to record, deactivate **Record all streams**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-menu-action-organizer-choose-video-record.png)
5. Choose the videos and click **Start record**. 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/tip.png) | The audio of all the participants will be recorded by default. |
    | --- | --- |
6. Click **Stop recording** when you are done. 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | The recording is available on your Apizee account. |
    | --- | --- |

| ![](../../.gitbook/assets/Apizee-see-also-icon.png) | **See also** [Download files - After the session in the portal](download-files.md) |
| --- | --- |
