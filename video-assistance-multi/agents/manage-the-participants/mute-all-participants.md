#  Deactivate the microphone of all the participants

{% hint style="info" %}
You are the organizer of the session and you want to mute everyone's microphone.
{% endhint %}

1. On the right, click the **Actions** tab ![](../../.gitbook/assets/button-actions.png)
2. Click the **Organizer** tab. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-actions-organizer-tab-selected.png)
3. If you want to mute all the participants, click **Turn off all microphones**. 
 
 ![](../../.gitbook/assets/EN-conf-menu-action-organizer-all-microphones-off.png) 

    | ![](../../.gitbook/assets/ok.png) | Every participants are muted now. The organizer is the only one that can be heard. |
    | --- | --- |
4. Click the button again to activate all the microphones.
